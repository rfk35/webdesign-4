About

Mutt Love Rescue is a non-profit, no-kill, volunteer dog rescue organization
serving the Washington, DC, metro area. We are dedicated to rescuing dogs
left homeless for reasons such as, dogs with high risk of euthanasia due to
pet overpopulation, dogs given up by their owners, dogs in danger of abuse or neglect.
